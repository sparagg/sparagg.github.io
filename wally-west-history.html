Wally West Flash Facts

EARLY HISTORY AS KID FLASH

Wally West was the Nephew of Iris West, then girlfriend of the previous Flash, Barry Allen. During and introduction to Barry Allen , Wally was struck with the same lightning bolt that granted Barry Allen his powers of super speed to become the Flash.

The Flash #110, by John Broome and Carmine Infantino

Shortly after the accident, he took up the moniker of Kid Flash and ran side by side with his mentor The Flash. Together they fought crime in Central City.

During his time as Kid Flash, Wally also fought along the side of the Teen Titans. They were a group of sidekicks that fought evil together. The other members of that group included:

  • Robin- sidekick to Batman
  • Wondergirl- sidekick to Wonderwoman
  • Aqualad sidekick to Aquaman
  • Speedy- sidekick to Green Arrow

TAKING UP THE MANTLE

During the Crisis on Infinite Earth event, Barry Allen sacrifieced his life in a battle with Anit-monitor. He then took up the mantle of The Flash in his place.
